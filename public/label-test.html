🐾 PLF Label Test Sheet

This page has a sample envelope sticker + receipt sticker + certificate for every species (6 orders × 3 pages = 18 pages). Open in Chrome → ⌘P / Ctrl+P → choose Save as PDF to export, or send directly to the printer. Note: envelope pages are 6×4 landscape, receipt and certificate are 4×6 portrait — if your printer driver doesn't handle mixed sizes, you may need to run separate print jobs per orientation.
