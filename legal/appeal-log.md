## How to Submit Each Appeal

1. Go to **TikTok Shop Seller Center** → **Account Health** → **Violation Records** tab
2. Click **View Details** on the violation with the matching Record ID
3. Click **Appeal**
4. **Reason:** Select "platform review error"
5. **Supporting images:** Upload the matching comparison image from `batch-2-comparisons/`
6. **Appeal Description:** Copy-paste the text above for that product
7. Click **Submit**
